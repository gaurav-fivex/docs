**Alerts** inform you about real-time changes across your **5X workspace**.

[Admins](/Team/rbac.mdx) and [developers](/Team/rbac.mdx) can remain proactive by integrating platform-level alerts to receive notifications externally via Slack or email.

## Connecting Slack

1. Go to **Settings → Alerts**
2. Click the **Integrations** button in the top-right corner

![slack-integration](/images/Slack_Integration.png)

3. You'll see "Slack isn't connected yet" message

![slack-no -integration](/images/Slack_No_Integration.png)

4. Click the **Connect Slack** button
5. A Slack authorization window will open showing:
   - "5X Alerts is requesting permission to access the [Workspace Name] Slack workspace"
   - What 5X Alerts will be able to view:
     - Content and info about channels & conversations
     - Content and info about your workspace
   - What 5X Alerts will be able to do:
     - Perform actions in channels & conversations

![slack-confirmation](/images/Slack_confirmation.png)

6. Click **Allow** to connect

## Setting Up Alerts

### A. Job Alerts

1. Go to **Settings → Alerts**
2. Click the **New Alert** button
3. Fill in the settings:
   - **Alert Name**: Enter a name (e.g., "Job Alerts")
   - **Type**: Select "Jobs"
   - **Jobs**: Choose the specific job
   - **Select Channel**: Choose your Slack channel
   - **Email**: Add email address (optional)
4. Choose notification triggers:
   - Toggle "Job failed" on/off
   - Toggle "Job success" on/off
5. Click **Set Alert** to save

![slack-confirmation](/images/Job_alert.png)

#### Alternative Method for Job Alerts
1. Go to the Jobs page
2. Click the bell icon next to any job
3. Configure the alert settings as above

![slack-confirmation](/images/job_page.png)

### B. Ingestion Alerts

#### Create a New Ingestion Alert
1. Go to **Settings → Alerts**
2. Click the **New Alert** button
3. Fill in the settings:
   - **Alert Name**: Enter a name (e.g., "Ingestion Alert")
   - **Type**: Select "Ingestion"
   - **Connector**: Choose the specific connector to monitor
   - **Select Channel**: Choose your Slack channel
   - **Email**: Add email address (optional)
4. Choose notification triggers:
   - Toggle "Sync successful" on/off
   - Toggle "Sync failed" on/off
5. Click **Set Alert** to save

![slack-confirmation](/images/Ingestion_alert.png)


## Delete an Alert
1. Open the alert.
2. Click "Delete this alert"
3. In the confirmation popup:
   - Click "Delete" to remove the alert
   - Click "Go back" to cancel

## Update an Alert
1. Open the existing alert
2. Modify any settings:
   - Add/change Slack channels
   - Add/change email notifications
   - Update alert triggers
3. Save your changes
